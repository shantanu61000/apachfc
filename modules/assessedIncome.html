Challenges of Informal Self Employed

Challenges
  • Absense of Financial Statements
  • Unreiable Financial Statements
  • Inadequate Banking
  • Overestimation of Revenue, Margin and Cash Flow
  • Underestimatition of personal and business expenses
What to confirm during Field Investigation
  • Assessablity
  • Checck on Business Expenses
  • Checck on Stock
  • Check on Cash Flow
  • Check on Documents
  • Confirm Ownership
  • Trade Reference
  • Neighbourhood check
  • Residence Verification Confirm other Income
  • Checck Personal Expenses

Field Investigation Coverage

Confirm type of business

Manufacturing/ Services/ Trading/ Agri (believe what you see and hear)

Check on Business Expenses

Rent, Elecctricity, Staff salaey, Telephone Trransport, Purchases - (Daily/ Weekly/ Monthly)

Check on Sales/Revenue

Daily/ Weekly/ Monthly/ Yearly (Business Income is never constant/look for seasonality - take lower figure - be conservative)

Check on Stock

(applicable for trading & manufacturing)Check for top items which constitute for 80% of sales. (believe what you see)

Accessibility

(Both Residence & Office) -It should be accessible on bike & foot (important from collection perspective).

Check on Cash

How much money debtors owe to him & how much money he owes to his creditors

Trade Reference & Neighborhood checks

Speak to his main supplier & customer to assess sales/purchase frequency/terms (depending on type of business)
Check in neighborhood about business/residence vintage/ favorable or adverse comments

Confirm Other Income

Rental, Tutions, Salary (many will be cash items only - do some market checks& be conservative)

Check Personal Expenses

Rent, Electricity, Telephone, Transport, Education, Medical

Residence Verification

Family size, Occupations, address confirmation, type & quality of dwelling, assets visible

Confirm Ownership
Check on Documents

See Banking/ITR, KYC in original for verification (basic compliance)

Assessed Income Based Eligibility

Revenue/ Income/ Sales Validation
  • Overall Sales Value
  • No. of Uits sold
  • No. of customer serviced unit sale price
  • Monthly/ Weekly/ Daily/ Annual Sales
  • Credit & Cash Sales
  • Peak & Cash Sales
Margin Validation
  • Sales price minus purchase price for key selling items
  • Gross average margin stated by the customer
Supplier & Buyer Verification
  • Amount/ Frequency of Purchase & Sales per month
  • Payment terms and adherence
Business Expenses Validation
  • Purchases (daily/ week/ monthly/ annual)
  • Credit and cash purchases
  • Rent
  • Manpower
  • Electricity
  • Telephone
  • Conveyance
Stock Validation
  • No. of units in stock
  • unit Purchase Price
  • Shell life of different type of items
  • Non-moving items/ wastage
  • Purchase ammount/ frequency

Informal Segment Success and Risk

Key Success Factors
  • Vintage of the business
    How long in same line of business
  • Length of key client and supplier relatioship
    How long are they dealing with the customer, what is feedback on timely delivery, quality of goods & services, payment terms and actual payment behaviour
  • Extent of Owners Equity
    What was the initial investment, and other capital infusion from him and family
  • market Reputation from customer, supplier, neighbored
Key Business Risk
  • On Revenue Line Increasing or decreasing trend Margin realization
  • On Cost Lineso increasing or decreasing trend
  • On Cash Flow increasing or decreasing trend Liquidity issues
