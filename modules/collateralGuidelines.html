Collateral Guidelines - Rajasthan

Properties Funded in Rajasthan:
  • UIT
  • Municipal
  • RHB
  • State Grant
  • JDA
  • RICO/LIC/AHWO
  • Builder Purchase
  • Gram Panchayat Patta
  • Unregistered Patta (Subsequent document registered)
  • Wall City Properties
  • JDA Approved societies
  • Technically Approved societies
  • Unapproved Societies
  • State Grant
  • 90B converted Properties
Properties Not Funded in Rajasthan:
  • Agriculture Properties
  • Kachi Basti Properties
  • Negative Society
  • Non-Transferable Properties
  • Properties in ecological zone
  • Properties built on reserved government plots
Doucumentation Requirement
Corporation & Authority:
  • Possession Letter
  • Payment Receipt
  • Agreement of sale
  • Lease Deed
  • Previous title document (Resale cases)
  • Permission to Mortgage
  • Allotment Letter
  • Sale Deed
  • No due from Authority
Gram panchayat & Society:
  • Patta issued by panchayat
  • Registered sale deed in favor of Allottee by panchayat
  • Original Site plan
  • Name transfer letter from Society ( Society cases)
  • 90 b converted orders ( 90 b converted properties)
  • Construction permission
  • Affidavit for land use
  • Misal Report
  • Grampanchyat NOC
