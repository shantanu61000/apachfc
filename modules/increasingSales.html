4 Success Mantras to be Effective Salesperson

  • Use the power of observation
  • Understand key businesses in your zone
  • Identify anchors & magnets
  • Identify “ success stories” in the zone
  • Observe when customer is busy. Compare the business with neighboring shops. Understand the difference in their approach.
  • Keep some suggestions handy based on your observations of market
  • Relationship selling requires observation and studying the business of customer
  • It is key to fix an appointment with customers and meet
  • First key step is to spend time to understand the needs of the customer
  • Relationship building is about need fulfillment and long term support
  • Engage the customer with success stories
  • Proposal and Chahat card to be given to the customer after analyzing the needs
  • Copy of the customer need profiling sheet has to be filed in the branch along with customer objectives
  • Mapping his needs for future use
  • Fix the meetings with the customer regularly based on the life stage requirements
  • Use special offers and new products / products modification as a relationship enhancer

Learnings

DO's
  1. Respect your customer's time
  2. Always wear neat clothes to the meeting
  3. Carry your ID card
  4. Introduce that you are from APAC HFC
  5. Understand customer needs & aspirations
  6. The relationship starts with hearing the needs of customer
  7. Spend time observing the customers prior to meeting them directly
  8. Explain our philosophy of offering customized solutions to our customers
  9. Know the customers needs and aspirations
  10. Seek permission of the customer to record data
  11. Start administering the Profile sheet to the customer
  12. Revert back to customer with proposal after filling the profile sheet and after you are convinced he could get a loan
DON'Ts
  1. Don’t go unprepared for any customer visit
  2. Don’t start the dialogue with a loan requirement
  3. Don’t assume customer’s requirement – ask him about his issues/concerns and tell him we can provide financial solutions
  4. Do not make ad-hoc presentation to clients, use flip chart for consistency and logical flow of value proposition

Referral Tree Creation Process

1. One customer has multiple linkages and you can strategically extract multiple referrals from him through a structured process by seeking the following referrals:

  • His customers / buyers / suppliers
  • His family and relatives
  • Neighbours
  • Service Providers
  • Family doctor, transporter, kirana store, etc
  • Friends

2. A customer referral tree can be huge and it may help you build a robust database

Reject Customer – Handling Process

  • These are influential profiles and hence rejections are to be handled carefully
  • May spread a negative message in the market
  • Rejections to be informed immediately
  • Delay will create an impression that we have very bad TAT
  • Reasons to be told have to be appropriate – move the blame to central team
  • Don’t make any false commitments
  • Don’t offend the customer
  • Tell the customer that in case we have a product / policy suiting to your requirement, we will come back to you.
