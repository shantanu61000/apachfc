Customer Selection Criteria

Customer Selection Criteria
1 Target Customer First Home Buyer for AHL/ Self Employed looking for Business Expansion for MEL
2 Minimum age at the time of loan application If Income is Considered:
  • For SENP: 21 Years. Co-Applicant – 21 years.
  • For SEP 24 Years. Co-Applicant – 21 years
  • For Formal Salaried applicants: 21 Years. Co-Applicant -21 years
  • For Cash Salaried applicants: 21 Years. Co-Applicant 21 years
  • If Income is Not Considered: 18 Years.
3 Max Age at the time of Maturity of Loan If Income is Considered:
  • For Formal Salaried and Cash Salaried: Retirement age or 65 years, whichever is lesser.
  • For SEP or SENP: 70 Years.
  • If Income Not Considered: 80 Years.
4 Geo Limit 50 KM from branch office
